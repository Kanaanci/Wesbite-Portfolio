Robotics Project

Here is a simple project I did as a Freshman. This is my first time working with AI or Robotics. This robot was made out of lego like pieces with the use of three motors, a light sensor, and a proximity sensor. My robot would travel forward until it was about 2 inches away from an object. After coming up to an object, the light sensor would determine what color the object was and, if it was blue, it would slowly move forward until close enough to pick up with item with its claw. If successful it would back up, spin around, emit applause audio, and drop the object. However, if the item was a different color, the robot would immediately back up, cry, and shut down.
